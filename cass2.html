OUR TEAM

We are a team of passionate student researchers from Cebu Technological University dedicated to creating practical and sustainable furniture solutions. Our combined expertise in design and research fuels the development of innovative products like the Adjustable Storage Shelf.

OUR MISSION

Our mission is to design space-saving furniture that adapts to the needs of students, renters, and homeowners. We strive to create products that are functional, eco-friendly, durable, and user-friendly, contributing to smarter living solutions.
