PRODUCT DESCRIPTION

The Adjustable Storage Shelf is a customizable furniture piece that offers versatility and flexibility. Featuring modular adjustable sections, smooth sliding mechanisms, and a lightweight assembly structure, it is ideal for small spaces.

    Key Points:

  • Space-saving design
  • Adjustable shelves for various needs
  • Easy assembly and disassembly

FEATURES

  • Adjustable Storage Levels: Customize the height and layout.
  • Compact Design: Perfect for tight spaces such as dormitories and apartments.
  • Durable Materials: Combines both high-quality, eco-friendly boards with scratch-resistant coating.
  • Smooth Sliding Mechanism: Easy to roll and reposition shelf components.
  • Portability: Lightweight structure for easy transport.

CARE TIPS

  • Clean with a soft dry cloth. Avoid harsh chemicals.
  • Tighten bolts and screws periodically for durability.
  • Avoid exposing to excessive moisture or humidity.
  • For reliable long-term maintenance, apply a small amount of furniture polish monthly.
